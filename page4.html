Cardiology

Our Cardiology Department provides advanced care for patients with heart and vascular conditions. We offer comprehensive services including preventive heart check-ups, diagnosis and treatment of hypertension, arrhythmia, coronary artery disease, and heart failure. With state-of-the-art diagnostic tools and highly skilled cardiologists, we are committed to delivering personalized care to improve heart health and enhance quality of life.
